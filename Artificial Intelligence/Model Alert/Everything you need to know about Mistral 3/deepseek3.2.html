Mistral 3 is a new family of advanced, open-weight AI models from the French company Mistral AI. It includes large-scale frontier models and smaller models optimized for local and edge computing-9. All models are released under the Apache 2.0 license, making them open and accessible for both commercial and non-commercial use-1.

The Mistral 3 family is designed for a wide range of advanced applications:

  • Multimodal Understanding: All models can process and understand both text and images-1-6.

  • Multilingual Proficiency: They support dozens of languages, including English, French, Chinese, and Arabic, with strong non-English performance noted-1-6.

  • Agentic & Function Calling: Excels at using tools (e.g., calling a calculator function) and outputting structured JSON for complex workflows-6-8.

  • Efficient Architecture: The MoE design of Mistral Large 3 means it only activates 41 billion parameters per task, making it faster and more cost-effective than dense models of comparable size-1-4.
