Several major freelance platforms allow registration as an agency or team, enabling groups to bid for projects and collaborate under a unified brand.upwork+2

Platforms Supporting Agency or Team Accounts

  • Upwork: Directly supports agency accounts; one can create an agency profile, invite team members, and manage projects collectively with an Agency Plus subscription.upwork

  • Freelancer.com: Allows users to set up teams under one account so agencies can compete for jobs together, share earnings, and collaborate on projects.linkedin+1

  • Guru: Offers the ability to form “WorkRooms” where agencies or teams can collaborate on projects, track deliverables, and get paid as a group.ddiy

  • PeoplePerHour: Agencies can apply and operate under unified branding, allowing multiple professionals to work together on client projects.peopleperhour

Additional Notes

  • Some platforms like Fiverr and Toptal are more focused on individual freelancers, though Fiverr Business and specialized programs may support agency-style collaboration for select service categories.fiverr

  • Regional platforms and niche boards rarely have built-in agency support, but some may allow for shared profiles or project management features for multiple users.
